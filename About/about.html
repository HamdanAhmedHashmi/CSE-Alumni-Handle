About US


The CSE Alumnus Handle is created by the students of Computer Systems Engineering department of Dawood University of Engineering and Technology. It is designed with the main pupose of helping the students grow and gain knowledge about the fascinating branches of the field of computers and stay up to date with the rapidly evolving technology.


The website is run by the senior students of the university, our alumni as well as the respected teachers. The seniors and alumni post blogs about important and new technologies and techniques in-demand in the professional life, which are then run by the supervisors and moderators.


Students are able to learn about new technologies and the on going trends related to computer technology round the globe. They also gain industrial knowledge and the alumni share their experiences via blogs and the students can also inquire about their problems and ask for help via comment sections the moderators and alumni then answer their questions or get in touch to resolve their issues.
